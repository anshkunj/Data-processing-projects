# Problem Statement

A company receives large volumes of customer feedback in text format.
The feedback is unstructured and difficult to analyze manually.

### Challenges:
- Noisy text data (punctuation, stopwords, mixed case)
- Difficult to identify common issues
- No structured summary available

### Objectives:
- Clean and preprocess text data
- Remove stopwords and noise
- Extract frequently occurring keywords
- Identify common customer complaints

### Expected Output:
- Cleaned text data
- Keyword frequency report
